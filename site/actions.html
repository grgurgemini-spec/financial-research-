First 90 Days: Week-by-Week

2026-08 to 2026-10. These 12 weeks define your career trajectory.

  • Week 1: Register platforms (Outlier, DataAnnotation, Mindrift), create Upwork account
  • Weeks 2–3: Complete Upwork profile with 3 GEE demos; invite 3 reviewers
  • Weeks 4–8: Bid on 10–15 small jobs (<€200); aim for 5-star reviews
  • Weeks 9–12: Complete 5 Upwork reviews; ship 1 eo-monitor improvement
  • By 2026-10-31: 5+ Upwork reviews, €200+/mo platform income, eo-monitor live feature

Why This Plan Works

  • Salary track first: It's the 16-year lever. Everything else is secondary.
  • First 90 days are critical: Build the portfolio (GitHub, Upwork reviews, EO product) that makes a remote job possible.
  • Savings rate is the engine: Not income streams or investment genius. 60–70% of every euro goes to ETF.
  • The product is strategic, not desperate: Ship an improvement by end-2026, measure coldly. If it's not €300 MRR by mid-2031, kill it and redirect.
  • Lean FI (2037) is near-certain: You can downshift to part-time consulting there. Fat FI (2045) is a coin-flip, but upsidable with salary + product.
